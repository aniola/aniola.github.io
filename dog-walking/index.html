Dog Walking

Who

Hi! I'm Angel York. I've been around dogs my whole life, and have years of petsitting experience. I petsit:

  • Dogs & puppies
  • Cats & kittens
  • Chickens and other livestock
  • Assorted small creatures that live in cages & aquariums

You may know me from volunteering at the St Johns Food Share, the North Portland Tool Library, or the local Buy Nothing group. Or maybe you've seen me biking around on an unusual bike, growing food at Portsmouth Community Garden, or walking someone's awesome dog.

What

  • Dog walking - one of the best parts of my day, so I'd love to walk your dog.
  • Bike-walking the dog (for dogs who like to go for a jog)
  • Petsitting
  • Housesitting
  • Gardensitting

When

Winter openings now available.

Where

Since I'm hyper-local, I can reach your house no matter the weather. And I'm always happy to walk in any weather your dog is down for.

  • Portsmouth/New Columbia
  • University Park
  • Kenton

Why

Standard market prices are $15-30 for a dog walk or cat care and $50-115 for overnight (in-home) stays. However, your pet needs love no matter your budget, so pay what you can afford. Get in touch!
